## Out of Scope

Explicitly excluded. Documented to prevent scope creep.

| Feature | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| Multiplayer | Not aligned with calm, private play |
| User accounts | Adds complexity, privacy concerns |
| Cloud sync | Contradicts offline-first principle |
| Social sharing | Not aligned with core value |
| Timed challenges | Creates pressure |
| Leaderboards | Creates competition |

## Requirement Outcomes

### Satisfied (8/11)

All accessibility and polish requirements fully delivered. NumberPicnic complete and functional. The app meets its core goal of being a safe, accessible space for children with sensory sensitivities.

### Broken (3/11)

**LetterLantern** and **StarPath** were completed during Phase 1 but their files were subsequently deleted. This represents a regression where completed work was lost. These requirements are marked as "broken" rather than "incomplete" because the work was done but the deliverables no longer exist.

**Resolution:** Either restore the deleted files in a future milestone or officially remove these games from the product scope.
