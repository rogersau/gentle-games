# Background Music for Breathing Garden

Add your calm, gentle background music tracks here.

## File Requirements

- Format: MP3
- Naming: `track1.mp3`, `track2.mp3`, `track3.mp3`
- Recommended: Calm, instrumental, looping tracks
- Duration: 2-5 minutes each (will loop)
- Volume: Normalized (app will play at 30% volume)

## Track Suggestions

1. **track1.mp3** - Soft piano or ambient pads
2. **track2.mp3** - Gentle nature sounds with soft music
3. **track3.mp3** - Calm acoustic guitar or strings

## How to Add

1. Place your MP3 files in this directory with the exact names above
2. The app will randomly select a track when music is turned on
3. Tracks will loop automatically

## Tips

- Keep it gentle and non-intrusive
- No sudden volume changes
- Suitable for children with sensory sensitivities
- Avoid tracks with lyrics or loud percussion
