Frequently Asked Questions

Does Gentle Games collect any personal data?

No. Gentle Games collects absolutely no personal data. The app stores only your local settings (sound, animation preference, last pair count) on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Is Gentle Games free?

Yes. Gentle Games is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscription model.

Is Gentle Games open source?

Yes. The full source code is available on GitHub under the GNU General Public License v3 (GPLv3). You are free to view, modify, and redistribute the code under the terms of that license.

The app sounds are too loud / too quiet.

Open the Settings screen (gear icon on the home screen) and adjust the volume slider. You can also toggle sounds off entirely. The app respects your device's media volume as well.

How do I turn off animations?

Open the Settings screen and toggle the "Animations" option off. All flip and celebration animations will be disabled immediately.

What is Keepy Uppy easy mode?

There is an Easy Mode toggle on the Settings screen that lowers the rate at which balloons fall, making it simpler for younger players to keep the balloon afloat. The option applies only to the Keepy Uppy game.

How do I clear my drawing?

On the Drawing Pad screen, tap the trash icon (Clear All) to remove everything on the canvas. You can also use the Undo button to remove the last stroke or shape one at a time.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please open an issue on the GitHub issue tracker. Include your device type, OS version, app version, and a description of the problem with steps to reproduce it.

Can I contribute to Gentle Games?

Absolutely! Pull requests are welcome. Please read the contributing guidelines in the repository before submitting a change.

Contact

For questions not covered above, please use the GitHub issue tracker. We aim to respond within a few business days.
