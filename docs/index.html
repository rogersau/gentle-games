Privacy

Gentle Games collects absolutely no personal data. There is no account system, no analytics, no advertising SDK, and no network requests during normal use. The only data stored is your local app settings (sound volume, animation preference, last pair count), saved privately on your device using AsyncStorage and never transmitted anywhere.
